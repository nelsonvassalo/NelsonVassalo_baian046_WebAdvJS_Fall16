Hailing from the european West Coast and graduated by the University of Fine Arts of Lisbon, I have been working independently and in collaboration with inspirational minds in NYC, Lisbon, Munich, Brussels and Luanda .

Being a graphic designer and type nerd by default I've always wanted to go into the digital realm, and so here I am DT to try to make that step. My initial interests are in interaction design, creative coding and 3D, but I expect to get in love with a lot more along the way.
